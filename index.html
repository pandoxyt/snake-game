How to Play

  • Controls: Control the snake’s direction with WASD.
  • Eat food: Grow the snake and score points.
  • Avoid obstacles: Don’t crash into obstacles or the walls.
  • Enemy Snake: Avoid the enemy snake as it competes for food, you can kill him, but only if you are longer
  • Speed Boost: Press the B key for a temporary speed boost for 15 seconds, Attention! the speed boost effect the enemy too.
  • Game Over: The game ends when you hit an obstacle or the enemy snake.
